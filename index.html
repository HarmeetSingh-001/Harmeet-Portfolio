UPositive

This was a team project. The purpose was to develop a website for a client who founded the company/idea of UPositive. This was meant to be a way for the client to display goodies of the month, allow users to subscribe to the client's monthly gifts, and allow users to have a space to share their own findings. The website also had cat pictures since the client wanted to show of the cat. The client's overall purpose was to spread positivity through gifts and sharing experiences. My part of this project primarily consisted of developing the Forum Page to allow users to make posts.

Knight Shift Demo

Knight Shift was a game concept that I along with 2 other students, Brandon Le and Joshua Jones, came up with for a final class project. The game was created as a small demo. My work consisted of mainly two items. Buff Orbs and Animations. Buff Orbs: These were orbs that on contact would alter the stats of the player at random. Animations: Most of the animations were completed by me using free assets found in the Unity store or online.
